Guidelines


A. Promote truth and intellectual accountability.
B. Promote awareness of Biodigital Convergence related technologies.

Whenever anyone sees 'psinergy.link/abc123', they should assume they'll see a Psinergist-curated industry based research paper, journal, website article, journal, patent, or video, etc.


Please follow these guidelines:

  1. Psinergist researchers only. Please do not share your login.
  2. Please focus on shortening links that are topics related to Biodigital Convergence.
  3. Please limit the shortened links to fact-based content only.
  4. Any valid URL can be shortened. However, this service is intended for very long URLs, such as those ugly Google search links.
  5. If you have the knowledge to do so, please remove tracking tags in the URLs before you shorten them.
  6. Please try to refrain from shortening direct links to PDF files. Some browsers try to load the PDF file right in the browser instead of downloading it, which can be an issue for older devices.
  7. Secure URLs beginning with "HTTPS" are preferred.
  8. Please do not shorten offensive or malicious links.
  9. Do not shorten affiliate links and do not promote crypto-currency. If caught, the account will be disabled and shortened links will be removed.
  10. Please govern yourself accordingly.
