Guidelines

 

The intention behind this service is to promote trust and intellectual accountability.


When the public sees a link to 'psinergy.link/abc123', they should expect to see a research paper, website article, journal, or video, etc.


Please follow these guidelines while using this service:

  1. Psinergist researchers only. Please do not share your login with anyone.
  2. Please focus on shortening links that are topics related to Biodigital Convergence.
  3. Please keep in mind, when the public sees a link to 'psinergy.link/abc123', they should expect to see a research paper, website article, journal, or video, etc.
  4. Any valid URL can be shortened. However, this service is intended for very long URLs, such as those ugly long Google search links.
  5. URLs cannot exceed 2048 characters. The Google search links are ~1000 characters so there is plenty of room.
  6. URLs beginning with "HTTPS" are preferred.
  7. Unfortunately, the title of some websites cannot be acquired due to security protections. In that case, the Title field of a shortened URL can be edited. You may have to go to the website using the original URL and then grab the title from there.
  8. After creating a shortened link, a detail page is presented so that you can copy both the Title and Shortened URL together, so that you can paste it anywhere you like.
  9. It goes without saying, Please do not use this service to shorten offensive or malicious links. Yes, Biodigital Convergence is wildly offensive, but you know what I mean.
  10. Govern yourself accordingly.
